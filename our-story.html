Our Story

We first met through mutual friends during a weekend trip to the coast. What started as a simple conversation turned into hours of talking, laughing, and discovering just how much we had in common. By the end of that trip, we both knew something special had begun.

After years of adventures together—from road trips along the California coast to quiet evenings at home with our dog—Liam planned the most beautiful beach proposal. On a golden evening in Monterey, with the waves crashing behind us, he got down on one knee and asked the question that changed everything.

Now we can't wait to celebrate the next chapter with the people we love most. Thank you for being part of our story.

Jenna & Liam
