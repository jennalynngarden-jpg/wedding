Things to Do

Monterey is a beautiful destination with so much to explore. Here are some of our favorite spots to visit while you're in town.

Monterey Bay Aquarium

One of the world's premier aquariums, featuring stunning exhibits of marine life from the Monterey Bay and beyond.

Cannery Row

A historic waterfront street with charming shops, restaurants, wine tasting rooms, and ocean views.

17-Mile Drive

A scenic drive through Pacific Grove and Pebble Beach, passing iconic landmarks like the Lone Cypress.

Big Sur

Just an hour south, experience some of the most dramatic coastline in the world. Don't miss Bixby Bridge and McWay Falls.

Carmel-by-the-Sea

A fairytale village with art galleries, boutique shopping, and the beautiful Carmel Beach just blocks from downtown.

Carmel Valley Wine Tasting

Explore the sunny Carmel Valley wine region with over 30 tasting rooms just a short drive inland.
