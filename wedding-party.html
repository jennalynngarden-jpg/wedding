Bridesmaids

Lacey Davis

Maid of Honor

Lacey and Jenna have been friends since middle school, and in a way she's responsible for this all (she set up Jenna and Liam to go to senior prom together). She graduated from Cal Poly SLO in 2020 and went on to complete her PhD in Aerospace Engineering at Purdue in 2025. She now lives in Denver with her partner, Tyler, and her pup, Cassini.

Kelly Devens

Bridesmaid

The Stanford roommate gods paired Jenna and Kelly together their freshman year, and they were inseparable ever since. They frequently got mistaken for sisters, laughed at the same brainrot memes, and supported each other through every p-set and essay. Kelly now works and lives in New York with her fiancé, Liam, and her cat, Puddles.

Melina Walling

Bridesmaid

Jenna and Melina met at Stanford and connected over their shared love of poems, tea, and tall men singing folk music. Their senior year dorm room was always filled with laughter and even after COVID sent them home they continued to call each other every day. Melina is now an environmental journalist for the AP and lives in Chicago.

Karey Shi

Bridesmaid

Karey and Jenna met as volleyball teammates at Stanford and haven't stopped playing together since. Their friendship grew through many Tahoe trips, board game nights, and tournament W's and L's. Jenna has actually sworn a blood oath to always play on Karey's volleyball teams. Karey lives in San Francisco with her partner, Eric.
